Launching into computer science

Computing as a Profession

Reflections:

Computer science is a multi-disciplinary domain and it involves mathematics, statistics, natural science and formal methods.

  • The Computer Science Discipline is Technical, Applied, Theoretical and Practical.
  • Computer Science professionals contribute to many other disciplines and industries.
  • Computer Science professionals possess Technical, Cognitive and Leadership skills.

Skills of a Software Engineer Project Manager:

Apart from Ethics, which is an essential skill not just for this particular discipline, I consider that leadership is a critical skill to succeed in this role. Also, good technical knowledge in the field and knowledge of Project Management Methodologies will contribute to creating trust among team members.

Other essential skills are:

  • Negotiation capacity
  • Time management
  • Planning
  • Communication

Desirable skills can be:

  • Level of knowledge in a specific Methodology
  • Coaching
